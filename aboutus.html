About Us

Norbu Jamtsho Travels & Tours was founded in 2010 with a vision to share the enchanting beauty and rich cultural heritage of Bhutan with the world. From its humble beginnings as a small family-run business, we have grown into a leading travel agency, specializing in creating unforgettable and sustainable travel experiences.

Our journey began with a deep appreciation for the pristine natural landscapes, ancient monasteries, and warm hospitality that make Bhutan truly unique. We believed that by offering personalized and responsible tours, we could not only provide travelers with life-changing adventures but also contribute to the preservation of Bhutan's environment and cultural traditions.
