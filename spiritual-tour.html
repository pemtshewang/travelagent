    Embark on a transformative journey to nourish your soul and rejuvenate your being. Discover the sacred sites, ancient practices, and serene landscapes that have shaped Bhutan's spiritual heritage for centuries. Immerse yourself in the Land of the Thunder Dragon and awaken your inner peace.

    Tour Highlights

    Meditation & Mindfulness

    Discover the ancient practice of meditation and mindfulness through guided sessions in serene monasteries and retreats. Learn techniques to cultivate inner peace and tranquility from experienced Buddhist monks.

    Yoga & Wellness

    Rejuvenate your mind, body, and soul through yoga sessions amidst the breathtaking landscapes of Bhutan. Experienced instructors will guide you through various yoga styles and techniques, helping you achieve a balanced and harmonious state.

    Traditional Spa Rituals

    Indulge in traditional Bhutanese spa rituals, incorporating ancient healing practices and natural ingredients. Immerse yourself in the rejuvenating experience as you relax and revitalize your senses.

    Tour Itinerary

    Day 1: Arrive in Paro Upon arrival in Paro, you'll be greeted by our representative and transferred to your hotel. After settling in, you can participate in a gentle yoga session to help you relax and unwind after your journey.
    Day 2: Paro Sightseeing and Meditation Start your day with a guided meditation session at a local monastery, where you'll learn the basics of mindfulness and breathing techniques from Buddhist monks. Afterward, visit the iconic Tiger's Nest Monastery, a sacred site perched on a cliffside, offering stunning views and a serene atmosphere.
    Day 3: Punakha Valley and Spa Retreat Depart for Punakha, crossing the dramatic Dochula Pass. In Punakha, visit the majestic Punakha Dzong before settling into a luxurious spa retreat nestled in the valley. Indulge in traditional Bhutanese spa rituals and rejuvenating treatments.
    Day 4: Yoga and Nature Immersion Start your day with an invigorating yoga session amidst the serene landscapes of Punakha Valley. Afterward, embark on a gentle hike through the valley, passing by quaint villages and immersing yourself in the natural surroundings.
    Day 5: Meditation Retreat Spend the day at a tranquil meditation retreat, where you'll have the opportunity to deepen your practice under the guidance of experienced Buddhist teachers. Participate in group meditations, mindfulness exercises, and discussions on the principles of Buddhist philosophy.
    Day 6: Paro and Cultural Immersion Return to Paro and visit the National Museum, which showcases Bhutan's rich cultural heritage. In the evening, participate in a traditional Bhutanese prayer ceremony and experience the local spiritual traditions firsthand.
    Day 7: Depart Paro After a final morning yoga session, bid farewell to the enchanting Land of the Thunder Dragon, taking with you a renewed sense of inner peace and tranquility as you transfer to the airport for your onward journey.
