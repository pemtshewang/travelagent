Bhutan Cultural Tour

Immerse yourself in the rich cultural heritage of Bhutan by visiting ancient monasteries, witnessing vibrant festivals, and experiencing local traditions. Explore the Land of the Thunder Dragon and discover its unique blend of spirituality, architecture, and warm hospitality.

Tour Highlights

Explore Ancient Monasteries

Discover the architectural marvels and rich spiritual traditions of Bhutan's ancient monasteries, such as the iconic Tiger's Nest Monastery, perched precariously on a cliffside. Marvel at the intricate details and sacred relics within these sacred spaces.

Experience Vibrant Festivals

Witness the vibrant and colorful festivals of Bhutan, where traditional dances, music, and rituals come alive. Immerse yourself in the festive atmosphere and learn about the cultural significance of these age-old celebrations.

Immerse in Local Traditions

Gain insights into the rich cultural tapestry of Bhutan by participating in local traditions and learning about the way of life. Experience traditional arts and crafts, try your hand at archery, and savor the delicious Bhutanese cuisine.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Paro

Upon arrival in Paro, you'll be greeted by our representative and transferred to your hotel. After settling in, you can explore the charming town of Paro at your leisure.

Day 2: Paro Sightseeing

Start your cultural adventure with a visit to the impressive Rinpung Dzong, a massive fortress-monastery that dominates the Paro valley. Then, hike up to the iconic Tiger's Nest Monastery, one of the most revered sites in Bhutan.

Day 3: Paro to Thimphu

Depart for Thimphu, the capital city of Bhutan. Along the way, stop at the Buddha Point to witness the massive Buddha Dordenma statue. In Thimphu, explore the vibrant weekend market, the National Memorial Chorten, and the Textile Museum.

Day 4: Thimphu Sightseeing

Spend a day immersed in the cultural treasures of Thimphu. Visit the impressive Tashichho Dzong, the National Institute of Traditional Arts and Crafts, and the Folk Heritage Museum to learn about Bhutanese traditions.

Day 5: Thimphu to Punakha

Drive to Punakha, crossing the dramatic Dochula Pass. Visit the Punakha Dzong, an architectural masterpiece situated at the confluence of two rivers. Stop at the Chimi Lhakhang, a temple known for its unique fertility rituals.

Day 6: Punakha to Paro

Return to Paro, taking in the stunning landscapes along the way. Spend the evening at leisure or indulge in some last-minute shopping in Paro.

Day 7: Depart Paro

After breakfast, bid farewell to the enchanting Land of the Thunder Dragon as you transfer to the airport for your onward journey.
