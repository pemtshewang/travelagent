    Nature & Adventure Tour

    Embark on an unforgettable journey through Bhutan's stunning landscapes, from lush valleys to majestic mountains, and indulge in thrilling outdoor activities. Discover the natural wonders of the Last Shangrila and create lasting memories.

    Book Nature Adventure Tour Now

    Tour Highlights

    Trekking Adventures

    Includes trekking adventures through Bhutan's pristine landscapes, from lush valleys to snow-capped peaks of Gasa, and witness breathtaking views. Challenge yourself on the renowned Snowman Trek, one of the world's most difficult treks, or opt for easier trails suitable for all skill levels.

    River Rafting Excitement

    Get your adrenaline pumping with an exhilarating river rafting experience down Bhutan's majestic rivers, surrounded by towering cliffs and lush greenery. Navigate through rapids and calm waters, taking in the stunning scenery as you paddle along the crystal-clear waters.

    Wildlife Encounters

    Explore Bhutan's diverse ecosystems and spot rare and fascinating wildlife, including the elusive takin, the national animal of Bhutan, in their natural habitats. Join expert guides on safari adventures through national parks and protected areas, where you'll have the chance to observe exotic species up close.

    Tour Itinerary

    River Rafting: 3 Days and 4 Nights

    Day 1: Arrive in Paro Upon arrival in Paro, you'll be greeted by our representative and transferred to your hotel. After settling in, you can explore the charming town of Paro at your leisure.
    Day 2: Paro Sightseeing and Hike Start your adventure with a visit to the impressive Rinpung Dzong, a massive fortress-monastery that dominates the Paro valley. Then, embark on a scenic hike to the iconic Tiger's Nest Monastery, perched precariously on a cliffside, offering stunning views of the valley below.
    Day 3: Paro to Punakha Depart for Punakha, crossing the dramatic Dochula Pass. Along the way, stop at the scenic viewpoints and take in the breathtaking panoramas of the Himalayas. In Punakha, visit the majestic Punakha Dzong, an architectural marvel situated at the confluence of two rivers.
    Day 4: River Rafting Adventure Get ready for an adrenaline-fueled day as you embark on an exciting river rafting adventure down the Mo Chhu River. Navigate through rapids and calm waters, taking in the stunning scenery as you paddle along the crystal-clear waters.
    Day 5: Punakha to Paro Return to Paro, making stops along the way to explore local markets and villages. In the evening, visit a traditional farmhouse and experience the warm hospitality of a Bhutanese family over a delicious home-cooked meal. After that, the team will depart Paro
